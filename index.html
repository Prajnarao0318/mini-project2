Orange Juice:

Benefits: High in vitamin C, helps boost immunity, supports skin health, and provides antioxidants.

Recipe: Squeeze fresh oranges or use a juicer to extract the juice. You can also add a touch of lemon juice for extra flavor.

Apple Juice:

Benefits: Contains antioxidants, promotes heart health, and may help with digestion.

Recipe: Wash and core apples, then blend them in a blender. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve to get smooth.

Pineapple Juice:

Benefits: Rich in vitamin C and bromelain, which may aid digestion and reduce inflammation.

Recipe: Cut and peel fresh pineapple, remove the core, and blend the fruit chunks. Strain if desired.

Grape Juice:

Benefits: Contains resveratrol, an antioxidant that supports heart health, and may help regulate blood pressure.

Recipe: Wash and remove grapes from the stem. Blend and strain to extract the juice.

Cranberry Juice:

Benefits: Helps prevent urinary tract infections, contains antioxidants, and supports oral health.

Recipe: Blend fresh or frozen cranberries, then strain the mixture. You can add water and sweetener to taste.

Pomegranate Juice:

Benefits: High in antioxidants, supports heart health, and may have anti-inflammatory effects.

Recipe: Extract the seeds from a pomegranate, blend them, and strain the juice.

Watermelon Juice:

Benefits: Hydrating, rich in lycopene and vitamins A and C, supports skin health.

Recipe: Blend chunks of watermelon and strain the juice. Optionally, add a hint of lime juice.

Lemonade:

Benefits: High in vitamin C, aids digestion, and may have detoxifying properties.

Recipe: Mix freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and a sweetener of your choice. Adjust ratios to taste.

Mango Juice:

Benefits: Rich in vitamins A and C, supports eye health, and provides antioxidants.

Recipe: Peel and cut ripe mangoes, blend the flesh, and strain the juice.

Kiwi Juice:

Benefits: High in vitamin C, aids digestion, and supports immune function.

Recipe: Peel and blend kiwi fruit, then strain the juice. You can mix it with other juices as well.
